Privacy Policy

Offline PDF Tools

Introduction

Thank you for using Offline PDF Tools. This privacy policy explains how our application handles your data.

100% Offline Processing

All PDF operations (merge, compress, split, reorder, convert to image) are performed entirely on your device. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Data Collection

Our application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not have access to your files or any information about you.

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or third-party SDKs
  • No internet connection required

Permissions

We only request the following permissions to enable core functionality:

  • Storage Access: To read and save PDF files on your device

Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting services

Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any data, our app is compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device, your files remain under your complete control. We recommend keeping your device secure and up-to-date.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
