Offline Converter

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Offline Converter handles information when you use the app. The current version of the app is designed as an offline-first utility and does not require an account to use its core conversion features.

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

The current version of Offline Converter does not require you to create an account, sign in, or submit personal profile information in order to use the app's core features.

The app may store limited information locally on your device so that the app can function as expected. This can include:

  • recent conversion history, if you keep history enabled
  • saved or pinned conversion pairs, if you choose to save them
  • settings such as language, theme, precision, and unit preferences

2. How Information Is Used

Local data is used only to provide app functionality on your device, such as reopening recent conversions, preserving preferences, and improving day-to-day usability.

In the current version, core conversion calculations are performed locally on-device and do not require a dedicated backend service.

3. Data Storage

Data described in this policy is stored locally on your device until you remove it by clearing app data, uninstalling the app, or changing the related in-app settings where applicable.

We do not state that all local device storage is encrypted unless the underlying platform guarantees it. If you need device-level protection, please use your device's built-in security features.

4. Sharing of Information

In the current version of Offline Converter, we do not require a user account and we do not operate a dedicated backend for core app functionality. As a result, there is no routine sharing of your conversion history or saved pairs with our servers for the core app experience.

5. Ads, Analytics, and Future Services

If a future version of the app introduces advertising, analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, or other third-party services, this Privacy Policy must be updated before those changes are released.

If you publish a build that includes ads or analytics, you should also update your Google Play Data safety form, Play ads declaration, and App Store Connect privacy answers to match the actual SDK behavior.

6. Children's Privacy

Offline Converter is a general utility app and is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe the app has collected personal information from a child in a way not described here, you should remove the app and contact the support address listed in the store listing for the published app.

7. Your Choices

You can manage certain local data directly inside the app by:

  • turning off automatic history saving
  • turning off pinned or saved combinations
  • clearing app data through your device settings
  • uninstalling the app

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the support contact information listed in the app store page or publisher contact for the released version of Offline Converter.

This document is intended to be hosted as a public privacy policy page for the current app behavior. Review and update it before publishing any build that changes data collection, advertising, analytics, or account features.
